More

The more items you add, the more Regi helps you get the most out of all your stuff. Here are the three ways how you can build up your MyStuff list quickly and easily:

FASTEST & EASIEST: RegiSync email.

RegiSync your email to find electronic sales receipts. Regi finds the items from the UPC info on the e-receipts.

All online purchases and many in-store retailers will send an electronic receipt when you provide an email address. Hesitant to give it out because of spam that sometimes follows? Simply unsubscribe from their mailing lists.

FASTER & EASIER: Scan a product.

Use the camera in your phone to scan the UPC code on the item or packaging.

Don’t know what a UPC is? It’s the rectangle-shaped strip of black and white bars with a sequence of 12 numbers (10 inside, one on each side). If you’re having trouble finding a UPC, Regi will help guide you on-screen.

FAST & EASY: Text Search.

Type your item into the Regi database to find a product match.

For best results, include manufacturer/brand name and the model name or number. Can’t find the right match? Go to the manufacturer website to get the correct info, or add something similar as a temporary placeholder.
